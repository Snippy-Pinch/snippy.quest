They're Back

In 3049, mysterious forces attack the Inner Sphere's rimward borders with superior technology and genetically engineered warriors. These are the Clans ‚Äî descendants of Kerensky's SLDF who left 250 years ago. They've come to conquer their birthright.

Who Are the Clans?

After the Exodus, General Kerensky's followers settled beyond known space. His son Nicholas Kerensky reformed them into 20 Clans ‚Äî warrior societies based on:

  • Genetic breeding programs (warriors are bred for combat)
  • Trial by combat (disputes settled through formalized duels)
  • Caste system (Warriors, Scientists, Merchants, Laborers, Bandits)
  • Bidding system (honor comes from winning with minimal forces)
  • Technological superiority (they never lost Star League tech)

What Makes Clan 'Mechs Superior?

  • Extended Range (ER) weapons shoot farther
  • Double Heat Sinks dissipate heat twice as fast
  • Ferro-Fibrous Armor provides more protection for less weight
  • Endo-Steel Structure lighter internal skeleton
  • OmniMech Technology ‚Äî modular weapon pods allow field reconfiguration

A Clan Medium 'Mech can often defeat an Inner Sphere Heavy through superior technology alone.

üîë Key Concepts to Know

LosTech (Lost Technology)

Advanced Star League-era equipment lost during the Succession Wars. Includes:

  • Double Heat Sinks
  • Extended Range weapons
  • Ferro-Fibrous Armor
  • XL Engines
  • Pulse Lasers

Finding functional LosTech is like finding a cache of medieval weapons in a post-apocalypse.

ComStar

Quasi-religious organization controlling all interstellar communication via HPG (Hyperpulse Generator) network. They:

  • Maintain neutrality among Great Houses (officially)
  • Preserve Star League knowledge in secret
  • Worship the memory of technology
  • Eventually reveal they've been secretly building a military force

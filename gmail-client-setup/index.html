Gmail "Send Mail As" Setup

Send emails from your custom domain using Gmail

What This Does

Gmail's "Send mail as" feature lets you send emails from a custom email address (like you@yourdomain.com) while managing everything in Gmail's interface.

Example: You have yourname@gmail.com but want emails to show you@yourdomain.com as the sender.

Before You Start

‚ö†Ô∏è IMPORTANT: Backend Setup Required First

Your IT administrator must complete these steps BEFORE you can proceed:

  • Configure email forwarding (e.g., Cloudflare Email Routing)
  • Verify your Gmail address as a forwarding destination
  • Update DNS SPF record to include Google
  • Test that forwarding works

Ask your admin to confirm these are done before continuing!

What You Need

  • Gmail account (e.g., yourname@gmail.com)
  • Custom email address that's been set up for you (e.g., you@yourdomain.com)
  • 2-Factor Authentication enabled on your Gmail (required for next step)

Setup Steps

1Enable 2-Factor Authentication

This is required to generate the App Password you'll need in Step 2.

  1. Go to myaccount.google.com/security
  2. Click "2-Step Verification"
  3. Follow the setup wizard
  4. Once enabled, proceed to Step 2
üí° Note: If you already have 2FA enabled, skip to Step 2.

2Generate Gmail App Password

You'll need a special password (not your regular Gmail password) for SMTP authentication.

  1. Go to myaccount.google.com/apppasswords
  2. Sign in if prompted
  3. Select app: Mail
  4. Select device: Other (Custom name)
  5. Enter a name like: Send as [yourdomain.com]
  6. Click Generate
  7. Copy the 16-character password (looks like xxxx xxxx xxxx xxxx)
  8. Keep this handy ‚Äî you'll paste it in Step 4
‚ö†Ô∏è Important: This password is shown only once! Copy it before closing the window.

3Open Gmail Settings

  1. Go to Gmail
  2. Click the gear icon ‚öôÔ∏è in the top right
  3. Click "See all settings"
  4. Go to the "Accounts and Import" tab

4Add Your Custom Email Address

  1. Find the section "Send mail as:"
  2. Click "Add another email address"
  3. A popup window will appear
  4. Enter:
    • Name: How you want your name to appear (e.g., "John Smith")
    • Email address: Your custom email (e.g., you@yourdomain.com)
  5. Uncheck "Treat as an alias" if you want replies to go to your custom address
  6. Click "Next Step"

5Configure SMTP Settings

Gmail will ask how to send mail. Use these settings:

  • SMTP Server: smtp.gmail.com
  • Port: 587
  • Username: Your Gmail address (e.g., yourname@gmail.com)
  • Password: Paste the App Password from Step 2 (remove spaces if needed)
  • Secured connection using TLS: Make sure this is selected
‚ö†Ô∏è Common mistake: Use your Gmail address as username, not the custom email. Use the App Password, not your regular Gmail password.

Click "Add Account"

6Verify Your Email Address

Gmail will send a verification email to your custom address.

  1. Check your Gmail inbox (email forwarding will send it there)
  2. Look for email from mail-noreply@google.com
  3. Subject: "Gmail Confirmation - Send Mail as [your custom email]"
  4. Either:
    • Click the verification link in the email, OR
    • Copy the confirmation code and paste it into Gmail's popup
  5. Click Verify
üí° Tip: If you have email forwarding set up (which you should), the verification email arrives in your main Gmail inbox within 1-2 minutes.

7Start Sending from Your Custom Address

Once verified, you can send emails from your custom address:

  1. Compose a new email in Gmail
  2. Look at the top of the compose window ‚Äî you'll see: From: [your email ‚ñº]
  3. Click the dropdown to select which address to send from
  4. Choose your custom email address
  5. Send as normal!
üí° Optional: To make your custom address the default, go back to Settings ‚Üí Accounts and Import ‚Üí Send mail as, and click "make default" next to your custom address.

Troubleshooting

"Authentication error. Check your username and password."

  • Make sure you're using the App Password, not your regular Gmail password
  • App Password should be 16 characters, no spaces
  • Username should be your Gmail address, not the custom email
  • Verify 2FA is enabled on your Google account
  • Try regenerating the App Password and using the new one

"From dropdown doesn't appear"

  • Refresh Gmail (press F5 or Ctrl+R)
  • Verify the email was successfully added: Settings ‚Üí Accounts and Import ‚Üí Send mail as
  • You need at least 2 addresses configured for the dropdown to show

Verification email not arriving

  • Check your spam/junk folder
  • Wait 5-10 minutes (sometimes delayed)
  • Verify email forwarding is working (have your admin check)
  • Try clicking "Resend verification" in Gmail settings

Emails landing in recipient's spam

  • Contact your admin ‚Äî DNS SPF record may not be configured correctly
  • Send a few test emails and have recipients mark them "Not Spam"
  • Wait 24-48 hours for DNS changes to propagate

How It Works

When you send an email using your custom address:

  • Gmail sends the email through Google's servers
  • The "From" field shows your custom email address
  • Replies come back to your custom address
  • Email forwarding sends replies to your Gmail inbox
  • You manage everything from Gmail's interface

Result: Professional custom email address with Gmail's powerful features!

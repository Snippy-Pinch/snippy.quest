Deep Discounts on Prescription Drugs for American Patients
Launched January 15, 2026
TrumpRx.gov is President Donald J. Trump's prescription drug discount program that allows U.S. patients with valid prescriptions to access deep discounts on select high-cost branded drugs at prices aligned with the lowest in other developed nations via most-favored-nation (MFN) pricing deals.
